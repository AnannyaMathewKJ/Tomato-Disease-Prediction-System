About Our System

This system is designed to assist farmers and gardeners in quickly identifying common diseases in tomato plants. By analyzing an uploaded image of a tomato leaf, our advanced model can provide a fast and reliable prediction, helping to ensure the health of your crops.

Our goal is to provide an easy-to-use tool that contributes to sustainable agriculture by enabling early detection and intervention, reducing crop loss and the need for excessive chemical treatments.
